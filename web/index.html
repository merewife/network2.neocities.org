Welcome to Network2

n2 is the revival of Neocities' first and most influential webring, The Network Neighborhood.

We created n2 because we believe the static web deserves to stay alive, and to stay fun. Network2 is a web collective - just a group of people with a common goal, creating websites and communities for the static web.
